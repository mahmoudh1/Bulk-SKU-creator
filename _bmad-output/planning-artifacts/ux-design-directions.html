UX Direction Showcase

Bulk-SKU-Creator design directions

Six visual directions exploring how a SaaS operations product can make dense batch workflows feel calm, legible, and trustworthy. Each variation applies the same product strategy with different hierarchy, density, and interaction emphasis.

Evaluation Lens
Core testDoes the batch become understandable fast?
Trust cueDoes AI feel constrained and inspectable?
DensityCan operators scan many rows without fatigue?
SupportabilityDoes row detail feel diagnostic, not decorative?

Directions

Direction 1 is the strongest default fit for MVP. The others test whether more editorial, board-like, or high-contrast approaches unlock better clarity.

Recommended direction
  • Use Direction 1: Triage Console as the primary product direction.
  • Borrow the guided prioritization banner from Direction 2.
  • Borrow the reason-chain side panel from Direction 3 for support and blocked-row states.
  • Avoid Direction 4 as the main UI; it is brand-forward but weaker for long-session legibility.
